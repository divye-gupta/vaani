# Vaani

- This project was bootstrapped with ReactJS
- Firebase is used to provide backend and additional auth support
- symbl.ai is used to provide processing background and AI assistance
 
## Our Vision 
We at VAANI aim to help people by increasing their self esteem , their self confidence and eliminate their fear of conversation by helping them to improvise their day to day speech habits, making their speech more effective and to the point fulfilling their purpose by clearing all the language negligence and paraphrasing them with our speech analysis.

### How this works?
We have a preset setting that help us to select the purpose of the speech giving an impactful view to the speech and then a process of analyzing the speech continues with the help of symbl.ai. The second step is matching the analysis results with the preset conditions if they are fulfilled by the speech and if the speech turns out to be on point. Third step is the last and the most important step which gives the special analysis reports and further points on the basis of which the speech can be improvised giving your speech an impressive shape.
